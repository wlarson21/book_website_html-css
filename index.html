William "Scott" Larson's Favorite Books

Posted 7/25/2018

Listed in this website are some of the most influential and useful books I have come across. There are both non-fiction and fiction books but both have shaped how I think. If you think you would also love these books, simply click on the picture and it will take you to Amazon where you will be able to purchase these books. Below is my favorite book.

The Harry Potter series is one of the best known series of my generation. Through 7 books you get the normal action, adventure and magic that you expect from the fantasy genre but you also get to watch the main character grow up and deal with the same problems that normal kids do. In addition it places great emphasis on learning, both book and street smarts.

Recent Books

  • On the Shoulders of Giants: Nicolaus Copernicus, Johannes Kepler, Galileo Galalei, Isaac Newton, Albert Einstein, Stephen Hawking

  • Harry Potter: J.K. Rowling

  • Fundamentals of Materials Science and Engineering: William D. Callister Jr.
