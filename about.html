About Me

Posted 7/16/2018

Hello! My name is Scott Larson and I created this website in order to share a few of the most influential books in my life. These books have helped shape the decisions I have made and have helped me become who I am today. I am currently an Quality Engineer working with Pratt and Whitney but I am working on expanding my horizons by also getting a Masters in Computer Science. One thing all the books I have ever read have taught me is the love of learning. Below is a list of all the schools that I have attended.

  • University of Illinois at Urbana Champaign
  • Northwest Missouri State University
  • University of Illinois at Springfield

The other thing that some of my favorite books have instilled in me is a sense of adventure. Whether that is trail running when I am at home or SCUBA diving and sailing when I travel. I am always looking for the next adventure, whether it’s in the real world or in the pages of a book.

Recent Books

  • On the Shoulders of Giants: Nicolaus Copernicus, Johannes Kepler, Galileo Galalei, Isaac Newton, Albert Einstein, Stephen Hawking

  • Harry Potter: J.K. Rowling

  • Fundamentals of Materials Science and Engineering: William D. Callister Jr.
